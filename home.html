The importance of a healthy diet

Diet is the basic condition for human beings to maintain life. To make people live healthy, happy, energetic, and wise, they must not only be satisfied with a full stomach, but also must consider the reasonable allocation of diet to ensure the intake of various nutrients required by the human body. The intake is balanced and sufficient, and can be fully absorbed and utilized by the human body.
